This is me

Hey, I'm Spencer Butler. I've always been drawn to anything with wires, screens, or code—if it runs on tech, I want to understand it. When I’m not deep into a project or gaming with friends, I’m out on the lake wakesurfing, trying to do tricks. I’ve got a weakness for K-pop, a bookshelf full of audiobooks, and I love spending quality time with friends, family, and a cat or two. I’m always looking for something new to learn and live life to the fullest!
